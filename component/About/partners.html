About us

City of Grand Rapids Community Development

Home Repair Services partners with the City of Grand Rapids to deliver critical home repairs and accessibility modifications to low-moderate income Grand Rapids residents. This work is made possible through HUD Community Development Block Grant funding.

City of Wyoming

We partner with the City of Wyoming to deliver critical home repairs and accessibility modifications to low-moderate income Wyoming residents.This work is made possible through HUD Community Development Block Grant funding.

Kent County Community Action

Home Repair Services partners with the County of Kent to deliver critical home repairs and accessibility modifications to low-moderate income Kent County residents.

Area Agency on Aging of Western Michigan

We partner with the Area Agency on Aging of Western Michigan to deliver critical home repairs and accessibility modifications to low-moderate income Kent County seniors. This work is made possible through the Kent County Senior Millage.

Michigan State Housing Development Authority (MSHDA)

MSHDA provides funding for the Housing Counseling & Education services of HRS, and helps direct homeowners to these important services.

Disability Advocates of Kent County

Disability Advocates provides professional occupational therapy assessment services for HRS clients in need of home modifications to improve accessibility to and around the home.

Senior Neighbors

Home Repair Services partners with Senior Neighbors to deliver critical home repairs and accessibility modifications to military veterans and spouses of deceased veterans. This work is made possible by the Home Depot Foundation, Meals on Wheels America, and Kent County Veterans Services.

AmplifyGR

Home Repair Services partners with AmplifyGR to deliver critical home repairs and accessibility modifications to low-moderate income Grand Rapids residents through the Neighborhood Strong initiative.

Home Builders Association of Greater Grand Rapids

The Home Builders Association of Greater Grand Rapids has been partnering with Home Repair Services for over 30 years by providing volunteers and material resources through its wide network of home building professionals.
