Lunch & Learn with us

Join us for a Lunch & Learn Tour

Home Repair Services continues to host monthly Lunch & Learn events in our
building located at 1100 S. Division in Grand Rapids. We invite you to join us for a
behind-the-scenes look at how HRS touches the lives of thousands of Kent County
neighbors each year. We look forward to inviting you into our home, sharing with
you how we strengthen housing structures and strengthen the people living in
them, and answering your questions about HRS.

The event is from 12:00 pm to 1:00 pm with a tasty box lunch provided.
If you have a few extra minutes, feel free to stay for a tour of our
converted 1940’s era auto dealership. Whether you are
new to HRS or have been a longtime friend, please be our guest.

If you have any questions please contact Angel Smith by phone (616) 241-2601 ext. 254 or email
asmith@homerepairservices.org.

Registration closes the Wednesday before the event at 10 AM.
