Home Repair

Our Repair Team performs critical home repairs that impact a resident’s health and safety. Examples include plumbing, electrical, roofing, heating, and more. A co-payment is required for each completed repair and is based on household income and total job cost.

Are you in need of a critical repair?
See registration requirements below:

  • Homeowner (owner occupied residences only) located in Kent County
  • Meet income eligibility requirements based on household size
  • Ability to provide the following documentation:
    • Proof of Ownership: A deed, land contract, mortgage document, or mobile home title
    • Your Michigan driver’s license or Michigan Identification card
    • Your most recent pay stub (if applicable)
    • Your latest federal & state tax return with all W-2’s, schedules, IRS Form 1099 for pension,
      or self-employment income (if applicable)
    • Your current Social Security Administration Annual Awards Benefit Letter (if applicable)
    • Food and/or cash assistance award letter (if available)
    • Your Friend-of-the-Court verification documents (if applicable)
