Ways to Give

Support

2,000+ households could not be served by Home Repair Services without your generous support. Your gift, no matter the size, will help build strong homeowners and ultimately strong communities.

Become a Change Maker

Join our community of monthly givers to make a significant impact in our mission by investing in homeowners. Your monthly gifts will help homeowners stay safe, achieve financial freedom, and learn skills to improve and maintain their homes.

Partner with Us

Is your organization looking for ways to give back to the community? We offer group volunteer and sponsorship opportunities for organizations looking to make a lasting difference in the community and in the lives of homeowners. We also accept donations of certain building materials that can be used on jobs. Reach out to learn more!

Planned Giving - The Dave Jacobs Legacy Society

Just as you prepare to protect your family's future, Home Repair Services must prepare to protect future generations by ensuring resources are available that lead to successful homeownership.

Volunteer

We are thankful for our volunteers who help with the construction of access ramps, home repairs, administrative work, and who build kitchen cabinets for families. Join our team of volunteers and see firsthand how counseling, education, and repair assistance can change lives for the better.

Lunch & Learn Tour

Do you want to learn more about the resources Home Repair Services offers homeowners? Join us for a Lunch and Learn Tour to get a deeper look at who we are and what we do.
