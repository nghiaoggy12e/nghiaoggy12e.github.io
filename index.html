Home Repair Services strengthens vulnerable Kent County
homeowners because strong homeowners build strong communities.

So others they can, too.

Repair & Access Services

We offer a variety of income-based services to support vulnerable homeowners. We do this by providing critical home repairs and accessibility modifications to those in need.

Housing Counseling & Education

Meet with a housing counselor to explore available resources to save your home from foreclosure, or take a workshop to help improve your financial literacy.

Homeowner Self-Help Services

We offer free home inspections, hands-on workshops, and kitchen remodeling guidance for those interested in learning more about home maintenance and improvement.

Become a Change Maker

Your monthly gift of any size will make a lasting difference every day in the lives of homeowners in our community through emergency home repairs, financial and foreclosure counseling, and do-it-yourself workshops.
